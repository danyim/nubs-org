About NUBS.ORG

Hi. I'm Daniel, and nubs.org is the first and only domain I've purchased in my lifetime.

I claimed this corner of the web on April 2003 and it has since served as the canvas for all of my wacky web design projects.

Throughout the years, this landing page has gone through many facelifts, which each significantly different than the last. While working on the current version of this site, I stumbled upon the artifacts of the old designs and found that though they may be extremely outdated, they still hold some historical significance to this site. So soon I will post a gallery displaying the "phases" that this domain went under.
